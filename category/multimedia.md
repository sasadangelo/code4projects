Turn a <b>Raspberry Pi</b> into a powerful home multimedia hub with step-by-step guides covering every aspect of the setup. This category walks you through building a <b>Raspberry Pi Media Center</b> from scratch using Kodi — including how to configure the media center, organize your media library, add YouTube support, transform it into a retro gaming platform with emulators for classic consoles like Nintendo DS, and even extend it into a <b>NAS (Network Attached Storage)</b> device.
