Understand and apply <b>software design patterns</b> to write cleaner, more maintainable code. This category explores the classic <b>Gang of Four (GoF)</b> patterns — creational, structural, and behavioral — and shows how to use them in real-world contexts such as game programming. Design patterns are language-agnostic solutions to recurring software engineering problems, and mastering them is a key step in growing from a junior to a senior developer.
